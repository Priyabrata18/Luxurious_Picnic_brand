Our Story

Founded in 2020, Éclat Picnics began as a passion project between two friends who believed dining outdoors should be as elegant and thoughtfully curated as the finest restaurants.

Today, we create magical moments for couples celebrating anniversaries, friends gathering after years apart, and families making memories together. Every detail - from our hand-selected linens to the seasonal floral arrangements - is designed to delight.
